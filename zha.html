探索花卉的奇妙世界，从拟人化到插花艺术，一切尽在这里。

In the garden of my dreams, where flowers eternally bloom,
You are the brightest rose,
a vision of love in full bloom.
Your petals soft and fragrant,
a symphony of colors bright, In your presence,
my heart finds its eternal light.

Flowers  &  Love

  当季风偷走花期，
         Monsoon thieves plunder blossoms,
我们仍紧握枯萎的隐喻：
  yet we clutch withered metaphors—
   爱是宇宙特赦的永生花。
Love: the cosmos' pardoned ever-flower.
